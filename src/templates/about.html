About LeafGuard: AI-Powered Crop Protection

LeafGuard is an innovative, AI-powered platform designed to assist mango farmers by providing instant and accurate diagnosis of common mango leaf diseases. Our mission is to safeguard your harvest by delivering rapid, data-driven insights directly to your hand.

The Technology Behind the Diagnosis

The core of LeafGuard is a state-of-the-art **Convolutional Neural Network (CNN)** model, trained specifically to recognize visual patterns of diseases. The application is built on a robust Flask (Python) backend, uses a MySQL database for persistent storage, and features a clean HTML/CSS/Vanilla JavaScript frontend.

Key Features for Modern Farming

  • Instant Diagnosis & Treatment Plans: Upload a leaf image from your phone or device and receive a disease classification, confidence score, and specific organic and chemical treatment recommendations within seconds.
  • Geo-Fencing Outbreak Alerts: Our system continuously monitors scans within a 5km radius of your geo-tagged farms and alerts you immediately if a significant regional disease spike (outbreak risk) is detected, allowing for proactive intervention.
  • Comprehensive Farm & Tree Management: Organize all your scans, history, and statistics by specific farms and individual trees to track long-term health and measure effectiveness of treatments.
  • Anonymous Regional Reports: Generate on-demand reports showing aggregated, anonymous disease distribution in your 5km area to understand local threats and plan preventative measures.
